# survivorrs
Survivorrs is a action themed rougelike survivor game in which you are a military officer who is surviving a apocalypse of monsters .
you have a gun with unlimited ammunatuion to which u have to kill those monsters to survivve 





## THEME
I have chosen endless theme because of this games core mechanies which is u have to survive waves and as u gusses the waves are endless like u can even survive
10^99 waves if u have skills to player 


## things accomplished
 1. A player with 8 directional movement , health ,and animations of hurt , die and walk, idle
 2. A automatic firing gun that always aims towards your mouse posittion
 3. Slimes that attack player with attack, hurt , knockback,dying animation
 4. A wave spawner that spawn waves of slime with number of slimes being ( wave number * 5 ) and some delay between ending of starting of another wave
 5. A wave indicator
 6. A background sound
 7. A basic looking tilemap
